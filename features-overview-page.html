THE LOOK

Set on a half-mile stretch of private white-sand beach dotted with palapa-shaded hammocks, the resort features 2 pools, a luxury spa, and a putting green, as well as a playground and kids’ club. The island resort offers 252 tropical-decor guest rooms featuring ocean- or garden-view balconies or ground-floor patios furnished with chairs and cocktail tables. Complimentary wireless Internet access is also available. Accommodations at the hotel include an ocean or city views, mini bars, nightly turn-down service, bathrobes, and slippers.

The coconut palms sway lazily in the tropical breeze. At this resort, you realize you’ve discovered paradise.
